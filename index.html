My Dearest Zumr,

12.21.25

Hey, Happy Birthday 🎂.

I don’t even know where to start, because wishing you happy birthday alone feels incomplete after things we came through and understand so much. It’s been almost 5 months since this University gave me a friend like you, and honestly, you’ve been one of the most constant people in my life there. Through random talks, positivity, negativity, teasing and laughing through screen — you were always there.

I know I’ve hurt you many times. Not intentionally, but I know intentions don’t erase pain. I’ve made stupid jokes, teased you when I shouldn’t have, and every time things got messy, you were the one stepping up. I am truly sorry fot that hurting part.

On your birthday, I don’t want to promise perfection. I just want you to know that I’m trying to be better — more thoughtful, more careful, and more aware of how my actions affect you. You matter to me more than any khana, joke, prank, or ego. I hope this year brings you happiness, growth, and people who never make you happy doubt your worth. And if I’m lucky enough to still be part of your life, I hope I can be someone who adds peace instead of confusion.

Happy Birthday. You truly deserve the best.

Your Innocent Dost 🐥
